Non-Surgical

Non-Surgical Options Tailored to You

Not everyone needs surgery. We offer a full range of non-surgical treatments to slow hair loss, stimulate regrowth and complement surgical results.

PRP Therapy

Platelet-Rich Plasma injections use your own growth factors to stimulate dormant follicles, improve scalp circulation and slow miniaturization. 3–4 sessions recommended.

From $600 / session

Peptide Treatments

Advanced peptides including GHK-Cu and TB-500 support follicle health, reduce inflammation and promote regrowth at a cellular level — physician supervised and fully customized.

Custom Protocol

Medications

Physician-prescribed Minoxidil, oral Finasteride and emerging low-dose options. We monitor your response and adjust dosing over time for optimal, sustained results.

Prescription Required

Red Light Therapy

Low-level laser therapy (LLLT) energizes follicles, increases blood flow and extends the growth phase of the hair cycle. Painless, zero downtime and highly effective for early loss.

From $150 / session
Advanced FUE Procedure

What to Expect During the Procedure

01
Local Anesthesia

Buffered anesthetic solutions minimize injection discomfort. Strict aseptic protocols and sterile instrumentation throughout. Your safety and comfort are our top priority.

02
Procedure Duration

Typically 4–8 hours depending on graft count. Larger sessions may be split across two days to ensure optimal graft survival and placement precision.

03
Recovery

Mild swelling resolves in 5–7 days. Transplanted hairs shed at 2–4 weeks — completely normal. New permanent growth begins at 3–6 months, full results at 12–18 months.

04
Aftercare

Detailed aftercare instructions, scheduled follow-ups and ongoing support from Dr. Choudhary's team. We are with you every step of the way through your final result.

Performed under local anesthesia  ·  Comfortable outpatient procedure  ·  Patients can relax, listen to music or watch content during the session

Your Journey

The Hair Restoration Process

01
Free Consultation & Scalp Analysis

Dr. Choudhary evaluates your hair loss pattern, donor density, scalp health and candidacy. We discuss your goals, design your hairline and create a fully personalized treatment plan — at no charge.

02
Pre-Procedure Preparation

Lab work, pre-op instructions and hairline design are finalized. You'll receive detailed preparation guidelines to optimize graft survival before your procedure day.

03
Procedure Day

Performed under local anesthesia at our Fullerton clinic. Most sessions take 4–8 hours. You'll be completely comfortable — many patients watch movies, listen to podcasts or simply relax throughout.

04
Recovery, Growth & Follow-Up

Transplanted hairs shed at 2–4 weeks (completely normal), then regrow permanently from month 3–6 onward. We monitor your progress with scheduled follow-ups and full aftercare support through 18 months.
