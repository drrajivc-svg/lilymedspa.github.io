Financing Questions

Will applying affect my credit score?
Cherry uses a soft credit check for the initial application which does not impact your credit score. A hard pull only occurs if you accept a loan offer.
What is the minimum amount I can finance?
Cherry financing is available for treatments starting at $200. Most med spa treatments and programs at Lily Med Spa qualify.
How quickly am I approved?
Most approvals happen in under 60 seconds. Once approved you can use your funds immediately at your next appointment.
Are there 0% interest options?
Yes — Cherry offers promotional 0% APR plans for qualified applicants. The calculator above will show all available options based on your profile.
Can I finance multiple treatments?
Absolutely. You can bundle multiple services into a single financing plan — for example, combining Botox, peptide therapy and a GLP-1 program into one monthly payment.
What if I have questions about my plan?
