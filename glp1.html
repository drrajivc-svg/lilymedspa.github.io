Common Questions

GLP-1 FAQ

How much weight can I expect to lose? +
Clinical trials show average weight loss of 15–22% of body weight over 12–18 months. Individual results vary based on starting weight, adherence, lifestyle factors and medication choice.
Are there side effects? +
The most common side effects are nausea, decreased appetite and mild GI discomfort — especially when starting. These typically improve within a few weeks. We start with low doses and titrate slowly to minimize discomfort.
How long do I need to be on medication? +
Most patients use GLP-1 medications for 12–24 months during their active weight loss phase. Dr. Choudhary will help you develop a long-term maintenance plan to sustain your results.
Am I a good candidate? +
Candidates typically have a BMI of 30+ or BMI 27+ with a weight-related condition such as type 2 diabetes or high blood pressure. A consultation with Dr. Choudhary will determine your eligibility.
