CEO & Founder · Medical Director · Lily Med Spa · Fullerton, CA

Dr. Rajiv Choudhary is a physician dedicated to delivering patient-centered medical care with a strong focus on safety, precision, and natural results. As the founder of Lily Med-Spa, he combines clinical expertise with an artistic approach to aesthetic medicine.

Dr. Choudhary's philosophy is simple: aesthetic treatments should enhance a patient's natural features while maintaining balance and authenticity. He believes that the best results are those that allow patients to look refreshed, confident, and naturally rejuvenated.

With experience in both medical care and aesthetic procedures, Dr. Choudhary is committed to offering advanced treatments supported by evidence-based medicine and modern technology. His practice focuses on individualized treatment plans — ensuring that every patient receives care tailored specifically to their goals and concerns.
